## 01. BRAND OVERVIEW

### Brand Essence
**Making Brilliance Normal**

Inala Digital Creatives (IDC) is a remote-first B2B Digital and Design Agency that combines high-end strategic rigor with the agility and pricing of a lean consultancy. We bridge the gap between expensive traditional agencies and unreliable freelancers, serving government institutions, SMMEs, and emerging enterprises.

### Brand Personality
- **Accessible Excellence**: Premium quality without pretension
- **Strategic & Agile**: Thoughtful yet nimble
- **Growth-Oriented**: Focused on client elevation
- **Authentic**: Genuine partnerships, not transactional relationships

## 02. LOGO SYSTEM

### Primary Logo
The IDC logo consists of three key elements:
1. **The Monogram**: Interlocking "IDC" letterforms creating a connected, flowing design
2. **The Arrow Icon**: A growth/uplift symbol integrated into the square mark
3. **The Wordmark**: "INALA DIGITAL CREATIVES" in custom typography

### Logo Variations

#### Full Color on Dark
- Use: Primary application on dark backgrounds
- Colors: Green gradient monogram + white wordmark
- Background: Charcoal (#2C2C2C) or darker

#### Full Color on Light
- Use: Primary application on light backgrounds
- Colors: Green gradient monogram + black wordmark
- Background: White or light gray

#### Monochrome White
- Use: Single-color applications on dark
- All elements: White (#FFFFFF)

#### Monochrome Black
- Use: Single-color applications on light
- All elements: Black (#1A1A1A)

#### Monochrome Green
- Use: Brand-focused single-color applications
- All elements: Primary Green (#6BBE4D)

#### Horizontal Lockup
- Use: Wide format applications (banners, headers)
- Monogram left + full wordmark + tagline right

#### Stacked Lockup
- Use: Square/vertical applications
- Monogram top + wordmark center + tagline bottom

### Logo Clear Space
Minimum clear space = Height of the arrow icon in the square mark
No text, graphics, or other elements should appear within this zone.

### Minimum Size
- **Digital**: 120px width minimum
- **Print**: 25mm width minimum

### Logo Don'ts
❌ Do not rotate the logo
❌ Do not change logo colors
❌ Do not add effects (shadows, glows, gradients beyond specified)
❌ Do not stretch or distort proportions
❌ Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
❌ Do not separate the monogram from the wordmark arbitrarily

## 04. TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Typeface: **Poppins**

**Headings & Display**
- Poppins Bold (700)
- Poppins SemiBold (600)
- Use: H1-H3, logo wordmark, impactful statements

**Body & Interface**
- Poppins Regular (400)
- Poppins Medium (500)
- Use: Body copy, UI elements, captions

**Specifications**
- H1: 48-72pt, Bold
- H2: 36-48pt, SemiBold
- H3: 28-36pt, SemiBold
- H4: 20-24pt, Medium
- Body: 14-16pt, Regular
- Caption: 12-14pt, Regular

### Secondary Typeface: **Source Sans Pro** (Alternate)

**Body Text Alternative**
- Source Sans Pro Regular
- Source Sans Pro SemiBold
- Use: Long-form content, reports, documentation

### Web-Safe Fallback
```
font-family: 'Poppins', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
```

### Typography Don'ts
❌ Do not use more than two font weights per design
❌ Do not use all caps for body text
❌ Do not set body text smaller than 14pt
❌ Do not use decorative or script fonts

## 05. GRAPHIC ELEMENTS

### The Arrow Icon
The upward-sweeping arrow symbolizes:
- Growth and elevation
- Forward momentum
- Strategic direction
- Digital transformation

**Usage**
- Can be extracted and used as a standalone graphic element
- Maintain 45° angle orientation
- Use in infographics, icons, directional cues
- Available in all brand colors

### Gradient Applications
**Primary Gradient** (Green to Deep Green)
- Direction: 135° diagonal (bottom-left to top-right)
- Start: #0e773cff
- End: #2d8f7aff
- Use: Backgrounds, overlays, accent elements

### Line Style
- Weight: 2-4px
- Style: Clean, geometric
- End caps: Rounded
- Use: Dividers, borders, decorative elements

### Corner Radius
- Standard: 8px
- Buttons: 4px
- Cards: 12px
- Consistency is key

## 06. PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGERY

### Photography Style
**Subject Matter**
- Real people in authentic work environments
- South African contexts and diversity
- Technology and collaboration
- Clean, bright, aspirational

**Treatment**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Minimal post-processing
- High contrast with clear focal points
- Avoid overly staged or stock-looking images

**Color Treatment**
- Slightly desaturated for sophistication
- Green color grade overlay at 10-15% opacity (optional)
- Maintain skin tone accuracy

### Image Overlays
When placing text over images:
- Use 40-60% black gradient overlay
- Or 40% green overlay for brand consistency
- Ensure text contrast meets WCAG AA standards

### Icon Style
- Line icons preferred (2px stroke)
- Rounded end caps
- Minimal, geometric forms
- 24x24px or 48x48px grid
- IDC Green or monochrome

## 07. APPLICATIONS

### Business Stationery

**Business Cards**
- Size: 85mm × 55mm
- Front: Logo + name + title + contact
- Back: Pattern or solid brand color
- Paper: 350gsm matte

**Letterhead**
- Logo top left or center
- Contact details footer
- Subtle watermark or pattern (optional)

**Email Signature**
```
[Name]
[Title] | Inala Digital Creatives

[Phone] | [Email]
www.inaladigital.co.za

Making Brilliance Normal
```

### Digital Applications

**Website**
- Hero sections: Full-width with gradient overlays
- CTAs: IDC Green with white text
- Cards: White with subtle shadows
- Hover states: Deepen green by 10%

**Social Media**
- Profile image: IDC monogram only
- Cover images: Horizontal lockup + tagline
- Post templates: Consistent layouts with brand elements
- Instagram: 1:1 ratio preferred

**Presentations**
- Title slide: Dark background + full logo + tagline
- Content slides: Clean white background
- Accent slides: Green gradient backgrounds
- Footer: Small logo + page numbers

### Print Collateral

**Brochures**
- Cover: Impact visual + logo
- Inside: White backgrounds, green accents
- Generous white space

**Banners**
- Vertical: Stacked logo
- Horizontal: Horizontal lockup
- Focus on key message + CTA

**Vehicle Branding**
- Large monogram on doors/sides
- Full logo on rear
- Website and contact clearly visible
- IDC Green must be prominent

## 08. BRAND VOICE & MESSAGING

### Tone of Voice
**Professional yet Approachable**
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Be confident without being arrogant
- Show expertise through results, not buzzwords

**Strategic and Results-Focused**
- Lead with business outcomes
- Quantify value where possible
- Focus on client success stories

**Empowering and Partnership-Oriented**
- Use "we" and "together"
- Emphasize collaboration
- Position as partners, not vendors

### Key Messages

**Primary Message**
"World-class branding and digital presence for organizations overlooked by major agencies."

**Supporting Messages**
- "High-end strategic rigor with lean consultancy pricing"
- "The missing middle between expensive agencies and unreliable freelancers"
- "Where exceptional work is standard, not exceptional"

### Tagline Usage
"Making Brilliance Normal" should appear:
- Below the logo in primary applications
- On marketing materials
- In email signatures
- On the website footer

## 09. BRAND APPLICATIONS CHECKLIST

### Before Launching Any Brand Material:

✅ **Logo Check**
- Is the correct logo variation used?
- Is there sufficient clear space?
- Is it above minimum size?
- Is it on an appropriate background?

✅ **Color Check**
- Are brand colors used correctly?
- Is there sufficient contrast for accessibility?
- Are gradients applied at correct angles?

✅ **Typography Check**
- Is Poppins used for headings?
- Are font sizes within specified ranges?
- Is hierarchy clear and consistent?

✅ **Imagery Check**
- Does imagery align with brand style?
- Is text legible over images?
- Are people represented authentically?

✅ **Message Check**
- Is the tone appropriate?
- Are key messages present?
- Is the tagline included where relevant?
